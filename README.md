# Renardet World Cup 2026 Sweepstake

A real-time sweepstake tracker for the FIFA World Cup 2026, built for Renardet staff.

## Purpose

Each participant has been randomly assigned four national teams. Points are awarded as teams progress through the tournament — the further your teams go, the higher you rank. Prize money is awarded to the top three finishers.

## How it works

- **Live data** is fetched automatically from the FIFA API every 15 seconds during matches, and every 60 seconds otherwise
- The **Leaderboard** ranks participants by points, updating in real time as results come in
- **Today's Matches** shows live scores and upcoming kickoffs
- The **Group Stage** grid displays current standings for all groups
- The **Full Schedule** lists every match in the tournament, organised by date
- All sections are collapsible; the page supports light and dark mode
